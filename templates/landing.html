Why AIpplicant?

AIpplicant is an AI-powered resume screening system designed to improve the hiring process for both students and companies. For students, it ensures fair evaluation of resumes based on skills, education, and experience, while offering helpful feedback to strengthen their profiles. For companies, it automates the screening process, filters irrelevant resumes, highlights top candidates, and reduces bias.

This leads to faster, smarter, and more efficient recruitment decisions, saving time and improving accuracy.
